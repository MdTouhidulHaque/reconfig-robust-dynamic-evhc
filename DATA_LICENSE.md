## Specific note on the DSS model

The DSS model used in this repository originates from the Iowa Distribution Test System associated with:

F. Bu, Y. Yuan, Z. Wang, K. Dehghanpour, and A. Kimber,  
“A Time-Series Distribution Test System Based on Real Utility Data,”  
*2019 North American Power Symposium (NAPS)*, 2019.
